# 🧬 Skin Profile & Personalization

## ✅ Status: COMPLETED

## Overview
Enable high-level personalization by allowing customers to define their skin concerns, enabling the business to send targeted, relevant rewards.

## 1. Customer Skin Profile
Add a new section in the **Profile** or **Settings** page:
*   **Skin Type Selection:** (Normal, Dry, Oily, Combination, Sensitive).
*   **Top Concerns:** (Aging, Acne, Redness, Hydration, Dark Spots).
*   **Birth Date:** (Already implemented, but critical for birthday rituals).

## 2. Personalization Logic
Link the customer's skin profile to the `Automated Campaigns` system:
*   **Targeted Rewards:** If a user selects "Sensitive" skin, automatically send them a welcome Scratch Card with a reward specifically for a "Gentle Oat Facial."
*   **Personalized Greeting:** "Hi [Name], we've tailored these treatments for your [Skin Type] skin."

## 3. Benefits for the Merchant
*   **Data-Driven Inventory:** See how many customers have specific skin types to stock relevant products.
*   **Higher Conversion:** Customers are more likely to redeem offers that solve their specific skin concerns.

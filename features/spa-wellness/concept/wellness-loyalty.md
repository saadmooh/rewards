# 🎁 Wellness Loyalty & Gamification

## ✅ Status: COMPLETED

## Overview
Elevate the Scratch-to-Win and Mystery Box systems to match the wellness theme.

## 1. Thematic Tiers
Rename the generic levels to something more evocative:
*   **Tier 1:** *The Glow* (Bronze)
*   **Tier 2:** *The Radiant* (Silver)
*   **Tier 3:** *The Zen Master* (Gold)
*   **Tier 4:** *The Wellness Elite* (Platinum)

## 2. Mystery Rituals (Mystery Boxes)
Instead of a "Package of Offers," winning a Mystery Box can be framed as winning a **"Secret Ritual"**:
*   *Reward:* "You've unlocked the Moonlit Lavender Ritual! Includes a 20min extension on your next massage and a complimentary herbal tea."

## 3. Specific Spa Triggers
Add loyalty triggers specific to service-based businesses:
*   **Off-Peak Bonus:** Scratch cards awarded for any appointment booked between 9:00 AM and 1:00 PM (Monday-Wednesday).
*   **Frequency Reward:** Automatically award a Scratch Card after every 3 treatments completed.
*   **Refer-a-Friend Ritual:** Reward both the referrer and friend with a "Shared Wellness" bundle (e.g., Two-for-One Mask Treatment).

## 4. Scratch Card Rewards
Include tangible, low-cost/high-value rewards:
*   Free 15-minute add-on (Scalp massage, foot soak).
*   Take-home sample kit.
*   Priority booking access for new season treatments.

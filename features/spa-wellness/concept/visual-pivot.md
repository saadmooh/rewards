# 🌿 Visual Pivot & Aesthetic Transformation

## ✅ Status: COMPLETED

## Overview
Shift the application's visual identity from generic retail to a premium, calming, and luxurious "Zen" experience suitable for high-end Spas and Skin Care clinics.

## 1. Color Palette (The Wellness Palette)
*   **Primary (Backgrounds):** Soft Sage (#E8F3E8) and Off-White (#FDFCFB).
*   **Secondary (Accents):** Rose Quartz (#F7CAC9) and Dusty Lavender (#B19CD9).
*   **Highlight (Luxury):** Champagne Gold (#D4AF37) for borders, active states, and premium tiers.
*   **Text:** Deep Charcoal (#2D2D2D) for readability, avoiding pure black.

## 2. Typography
*   **Headings:** Use an elegant Serif font (e.g., *Playfair Display* or *Cormorant Garamond*) to convey luxury and history.
*   **Body:** Keep a clean Sans-Serif (e.g., *Inter* or *Montserrat*) for clarity in service descriptions and prices.

## 3. UI Components & Feel
*   **Soft Blur (Glassmorphism):** Use `backdrop-blur` on navigation bars and cards to create a "misty," high-end feel.
*   **Clean Spacing:** Increase white space (padding/margins) between sections to avoid a cluttered look, promoting a sense of calm.
*   **Rounded Corners:** Use large border-radii (`rounded-3xl`) for a soft, organic feel.
*   **Texture:** Add a very subtle noise/grain texture to the main background (`bg-opacity-5`) to give a tactile, premium paper feel.

## 4. Iconography
*   Switch to thinner, more delicate icons (e.g., Lucide-React with `strokeWidth={1.5}`).
*   Use organic shapes and nature-inspired icons (Leaves, Droplets, Sun, Moon).
